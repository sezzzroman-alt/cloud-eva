Common Questions

FAQs

What exactly does Cloudeva.ai do? +
Cloudeva.ai helps teams understand cloud change and make better decisions. It explains what changed, who changed it, why it changed, and how decisions should be reviewed and governed. It sits above monitoring tools as a decision clarity and accountability layer.
Is Cloudeva.ai a monitoring or observability tool? +
No. Cloudeva.ai consumes signals from cloud platforms and tools, but its focus is decision context — not incident response. It does not generate real-time alerts or replace observability systems.
Does Cloudeva.ai make changes in my cloud account? +
Only when explicitly allowed and only in higher plans. Starter, Growth, and Pro plans offer read-only access. Enterprise requires human approval for any action. Cloudeva.ai never executes silently.
What does the EVA-O model mean? +
EVA-O stands for Explain → Verify → Advise → Orchestrate. It describes how Cloudeva.ai matures with your organization — from explaining what changed, to governing policy-bound, auditable execution.
What's included in the Public Preview? +
The Pro plan Public Preview includes context-rich FinOps & SecOps signals, cross-domain explainability, review-ready summaries, and AI-assisted explanations (non-executing). It's free until March 31.
